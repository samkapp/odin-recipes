Brownie Recipe

A very quick and easy brownie recipe that should only take 30 minutes. Made using very basic home ingredients, so it's very easy to bake!.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white sugar

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup butter, melted

  • 4 eggs

  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1/2 cup walnut halves

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C)

  2. Grease a 9x13-inch pan

  3. Into a large bowl mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt until combined.

  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan and decorate with walnut halves

  5. Bake until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the s ides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes

  6. Cool before slicing
